Dharmendra Datta and Ors. V Pipal Ltd
The applicant sought leave to appear and defend. Only one applicant swore an affidavit in support of the application and did not state anywhere that he had authority to swear it on the others behalf. The issue at court was whether the affidavit was incompetent.
Court: The affidavit was incompetent but it was counsels error which was not visited on the applicants.
