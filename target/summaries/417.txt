Sabani v Uganda  Division: High Court of Uganda at Kampala  Date of judgment: 16 April 1974  Case Number: 256/1973 (130/74)  Before: Wambuzi CJ  Sourced by: LawAfrica  [1] Criminal Practice and Procedure  Compensation  Robbery  Award mandatory and without  financial limit  Penal Code, s. 273 (3) (U.).  Editors Summary  The accused was convicted of robbery by a magistrate and in addition to imprisonment was ordered to pay compensation of more than Shs. 1,000/-.  On appeal it was contended that the compensation awarded exceeded the limit imposed by the  Magistrates Courts Act 1970, s. 209 (1).  Held  compensation must be awarded on a conviction of robbery and the amount is not limited.  Appeal dismissed.  No cases referred to in judgment
