FACTS
-.The brief facts giving rise to the suit are that at all material times from November 1997 to March 2001, the plaintiff was an employee of the defendant Bank in different capacities and resigned prompting the defendants to publish in the newspaper advising any persons dealing with the plaintiffs to do so at their own risk. The plaintiffs averred that this was defamation and sued

ISSUES
Whether the advertisement of 23/3/2001 was defamatory of the plaintiff.
Whether the plaintiff is entitled to remedies sought in the plaint.

HELD
-normal course of event for the defendant to warn its customers of their former employee whom they suspected was threatening the business of the bank thus defamation was ruled out.
-no remedies deserved
