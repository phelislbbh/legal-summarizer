Ugali Products Ltd v Republic

Division: High Court of Kenya at Mombasa

Date of judgment: 25 October 1974

Case Number: 59/1974 (121/74)

Before: Sir Dermot Sheridan J

Sourced by: LawAfrica

[1] Criminal Practice and Procedure  Forfeiture  Maize  Notice under Criminal Procedure Code must be given  Maize Marketing Act (Cap. 338), s. 43(K.)  Criminal Procedure Code s. 389A (K.).

Editors Summary

On convicting an accused of moving maize unlawfully, the magistrate made an order for its forfeiture without serving notice on the applicant its owner.

On application to set aside the order, the respondent argued that as a procedure is laid down for the forfeiture of anything but maize, no notice was required.

Held  the general provisions on forfeiture in the Criminal Procedure Code apply to the forfeiture of maize.

Application allowed.
