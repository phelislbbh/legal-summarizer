BEFORE: THE HON. MR. JUSTICE GEOFFREY KIRYABWIRE.


FACTS
The Plaintiffs claim against the defendant is for the recovery of US$43,838 being the cost of salt supplied to the defendants but not paid for. The plaintiff also seeks general damages for breach of contract.

ISSUES
Whether the defendant did any business with the plaintiff?
Whether a sum of US$43,838 was outstanding and owing from the said business?
What remedies are available to the parties.

HELD
-Court from the evidence at hand was convinced that the plaintiff and the defendant has a business relationship
-That indeed the said sum was outstanding from the said business relationship
-Special Damages and interest awarded
