Virji and others v Sood (No. 2)  Division: High Court of Kenya at Nairobi  Date of judgment: 4 August 1972  Date of judgment: 4 August 1972  Case Number: 1536/1970 (111/74)  Before: Trevelyan J  Sourced by: LawAfrica  [1] Arbitration  Submission  Parol submission valid.  Editors Summary  The legal question for decision was whether it was possible for there to be a binding parol submission to arbitration.  Held  a parol submission to arbitration is valid and enforceable (Bremer Oeltransport v. Drewry (1) followed).  Order accordingly.  Case referred to Judgment:  (1) Bremer Oeltransport v. Drewry, [1933] 1 K.B. 753; [1933] All E.R. Rep. 851.
