Republic v Juma  Division: High Court of Tanzania at Mbeya  Division: High Court of Tanzania at Mbeya  Date of judgment:  Case Number: 175/1972 (52/74)  Before: Kwikima Ag J  Sourced by: LawAfrica  [1] Criminal Law  Murder  Provocation  Threat to kill by witchcraft  Not immediately acted on   Effect.  Editors Summary  The accused was charged with murder. He had killed his father, a reputed wizard, sometime after the latter had threatened to bewitch him.  Held  The accused had not killed immediately after the provocation and was guilty of murder.  Cases referred to Judgment:  (1) R. v. Kajuna (1945), 12 E.A.C.A. 104.  (2) Eria Galikuwa v. R. (1951), 18 E.A.C.A. 175.
