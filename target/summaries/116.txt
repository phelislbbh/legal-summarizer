Devshi v Diamond Concrete Co

Division: Court of Appeal at Nairobi

Division: Court of Appeal at Nairobi

Date of judgment: 5 December 1974

Case Number: 16/1974 (6/75)

Before: Law Ag V-P

Sourced by: LawAfrica

[1] Appeal  Out of time  No action for 3 years  Application refused although not opposed.

Editors Summary

The applicant applied for extension of time for filing his record of appeal, notice of appeal having been given 3 years ago and no steps having been taken in the meanwhile. The respondent did not oppose the application.

Held  the court was not satisfied that there was any reason why the appeal was not lodged in time.

Application dismissed.
