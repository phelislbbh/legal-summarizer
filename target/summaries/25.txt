BEFORE: HON. MR. JUSTICE LAMECK N. MUKASA

FACTS
Applicant had instituted suit against respondent rising out of loan payment complications. This application was lodged as the applicant sought the AG be added to the defendants for the 1st respondent was a entity of the Government

ISSUES
Whether the AG could be added to the defendants

HELD
Court held in the negative stating that the Government of Uganda was not privy to the loan agreement between respondent and applicant and dismissed the application
