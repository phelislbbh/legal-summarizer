Dave v Business Machines Ltd  [1974] 1 EA 68 (CAN)  Division: Court of Appeal at Nairobi  Date of judgment: 14 March 1974  Case Number: 45/1973 (24/74)  Before: Sir William Duffus P, Law Ag V-P and Musoke JA  Sourced by: LawAfrica  [1] Practice and Procedure  Attendance  Defendant not attending  Plaintiff must prove case.  Editors Summary  The appellant did not attend the hearing of the case brought against him by the appellant. The judge gave  judgment relying on letters which were handed to him but not proved.  On appeal  Held  where a defence has been filed but the defendant does not attend, the plaintiff must prove his case  by evidence.  Appeal allowed.  Case referred to Judgment:  (1) Eksteen v. Kutosi (1951), 24 (2) K.L.R. 90.
