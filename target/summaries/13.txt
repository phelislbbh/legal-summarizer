[An appeal from the sentence of the High Court at Fort Portal
(Zahurikiza, J.) dated 26/11/2003 in Criminal Session Case
No. 59 of 2002]


FACTS
Agaba Job, the appellant, has appealed with leave of this court against the sentence of 10 years imprisonment after he was found guilty of defiling a 6 year old.

ISSUES
That the sentence of 10 years in such circumstances where the appellant committed the offence under the influence of alcohol, and where he showed remorse/repentance during trial and saved courts time was harsh. 

HELD
Sentencing power is a discretion of the judge and that in ordering the 10 years had actually considered the otherwise mitigating factors and had infect passed out a lenient sentence. Appeal Dismissed
