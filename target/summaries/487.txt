THE REPUBLIC OF UGANDA IN THE HIGH COURT OF UGANDA AT KAMPALA (COMMERCIAL COURT DIVISION) HCT-00-CC-CS-0154 OF 2005  UNITED BUILDING SERVICES LTD========PLAINTIFF VERSUS YAFESI MUZIRA T/A QUICKSET BUILDERS & CO. ====DEFENDANT   FACTS The Plaintiff M/s United Building Services Ltd, a limited liability company incorporated and carrying on business in Uganda brought this suit against the Defendant Yafesi Muzira t/a Quickset Builders & company arising out of a breach of contract.  ISSUES Whether or not there was a contract of hire of the construction equipment between the parties. If so, whether there was a breach of the said contract by the Defendant? What are the remedies?  HELD Court found that there was an existing contract Court further held that there was indeed a breach of contract Special and General Damages were awarded with interest
