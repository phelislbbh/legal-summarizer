Najeno v Serwanga

Division: High Court of Uganda at Kampala

Date of judgment: 29 March 1974

Case Number: 45/1971 (41/75)

Before: Allen Ag J

Sourced by: LawAfrica

[1] Probate and Administration  Succession  Succession Act applies to all persons.

[2] Civil Practice and Procedure  Defendant  Substitution for dead defendant  Grant of probate or letters necessary.

Editors Summary

The respondent was ordered to be substituted for a deceased appellant without producing to the court any grant of probate or letters of administration.

On appeal

Held 

(i) the Succession Act applies to all persons;

( ii) a grant is necessary to constitute anyone the legal or personal representative of another.

Appeal allowed.

No cases referred to in judgment
