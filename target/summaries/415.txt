MUKASA  FACTS -This application is for an injunction be issued to restrain the Respondent, Electricity Regulatory Authority (ERA) from granting an Independent Power Producer (IPP) license to M/S Jacobsen Electro As for the generation and sale of 50MW of thermal power until the final determination of Commercial Appeal No. 02 of 2007. -Applicants contend that they appealed the decision of the respondent to award tender to another firm other than theirs and that appeal has not yet been determined  ISSUES -whether the applicant proves the requirements for the injection to be granted  HELD Court in its findings was of the view that the applicant had not thoroughly proved that the balance of convenience was in his favour thus the application was dismissed. Court however, ordered that this being an urgent matter, the appeal of the respondents decision be fixed for hearing as soon as possible
