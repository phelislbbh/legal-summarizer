JUSTICE FMS EGONDA-NTENDE   FACTS The plaintiff brought this action to recover from the defendant Shs8,269,553,502.00, damages, interest and costs of the suit. The plaintiff contended in the plaint that it had ordered assorted steel from the defendant for the sum of US$29,033,900.00. The plaintiff contended that it had deposited monies on the defendants account for the steel and even paid Shs. 8,269,553,502.00 being the vat component to the defendants tax benefit. The plaintiff further contended in its plaint that the defendant supplied only a token quantity of steel and it refused to supply the quantity contracted.  ISSUES whether URA could be attached to the case to recover the sums in question whether plaintiff had a claim on the funds in question HELD Court dismissed the application that the plaintiff had no specific claim on the funds in question
