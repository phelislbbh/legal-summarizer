FACTS The plaintiff brought this action against the defendant bank seeking a declaration that he is not indebted to the defendant and that the mortgage purportedly created over his property by the defendant is null and void and should be removed.  ISSUES -Whether the suit cheque was dishonoured and if so, whether the proper steps were taken on dishonour;  -Whether the defendant was negligent in allowing the plaintiff to draw the proceed of the suit cheque at the time it did;  -Whether the plaintiff obtained a loan facility from the defendant;  - If so, whether the plaintiff executed the security documents under duress/undue influence;  - Whether the plaintiff is indebted to the defendant bank;  -Whether the defendant correctly exercised the right of set-off of the plaintiffs accounts;  -Whether the defendant bank is entitled to recover the US$88.294.81 from the plaintiff; (a) As a collecting bank upon dishonour of an instrument sent for collection and, or   (b) On the strength of the security documents.  -Remedies.  HELD -Cheque was properly dishonoured -Bank not found negligent to warrant liability -No loan agreement existed -Plaintiff indebted to the defendant -Set off of from the client's account was justified
