BEFORE: HON. MR. JUSTICE LAMECK N. MUKASA
FACTS
-Applicant sought leave from court to amend the plaint so as to include such information being material to the original suit and had been neglected by the former counsel for the applicant

ISSUES
whether applicant had satisfied legal requirements to be granted an amendment order

HELD
-Court was of the view that the applicant had proved that omission of some facts in the plaint exposed him to suffering grave consequences and thus allowed amendment of the plaint accordingly.
