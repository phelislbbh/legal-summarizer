Eugen v Hadija  Division: Court of Appeal at Dar Es Salaam  Date of judgment: 13 October 1973  Case Number: 29/1973 (1/74)  Before: Sir William Duffus P, Spry V-P and Law JA  Sourced by: LawAfrica  Appeal from: High Court of Tanzania  Kwikima, Ag.J  [1] Matrimonial Causes  Maintenance  Wife in desertion  No liability on husband to maintain wife   Marriage Act 1971, s. 63 (T.).  Editors Summary  The respondent wife applied to the primary court for maintenance, which found that she was in desertion but nevertheless awarded her maintenance. On appeal to the Court of Appeal the question was whether a right to maintenance in such circumstances was given by the Marriage Act 1971, s. 63.     Held   (i) a husbands duty is to maintain his wife by providing a home for her.  ( ii) a wife who rejects the home offered to her is not entitled to money in lieu thereof.  Appeal allowed.  No cases referred to in judgment
