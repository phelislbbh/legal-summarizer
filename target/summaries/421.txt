Salini Construction Co Ltd Applicant

Versus

Dr. John Nuwagaba Respondent

Legislation referred to:
Civil Procedure Rules.


BEFORE: THE HONOURABLE MR. JUSTICE FMS EGONDA-NTENDE

FACTS
The Applicant is the defendant in the head suit. A default judgement was obtained against it. It seeks to set aside the resultant decree on the ground that there was no service of the summons on its authorised officers and that it has a good defence to the claim.

ISSUES
whether there was proper service on the applicant
whether application cpuld be granted

HELD
Court held that on the evidence of the applicant not much contested, there was inadequate service upon the applicant and thus it could no be able to file defences to the respondents claim thus the application was allowed
