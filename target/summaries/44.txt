Bagologoza v National Parks Trustees

Division: High Court of Uganda at Kampala

Date of judgment: 18 February 1974

Case Number: 1026/1972 (67/74)

Before: Lubogo J

Sourced by: LawAfrica

[1] Negligence  Master and servant  Safe place of work  Whether provided.

Editors Summary

The plaintiff was employed by the defendants in a National Park. The area of the camp was lit, and the employees were ordered to be indoors by dusk.

The plaintiff was injured by a buffalo while returning from a bath house at night during a temporary failure of the electricity.

Held 

(i) A safe place of work was provided;

( ii) the defendants were not negligent.

Case dismissed.
