FACTS
 The applicant seeks orders that the warrant of arrest and committal of the judgment debtor be set aside and he be released from Civil Prison; the exparte judgment and decree be set aside; the applicant/judgment debtor be granted unconditional leave to defend the suit; that costs be provided for.

ISSUES
-whether applicant had been served
-whether the warrant of arrest was legal

HELD
-Court on analysis of purported service documents was of the view that many alterations n discrepancies existed therein and thus were not satisfied that the applicant was duly served
-Warrant of arrest set aside
