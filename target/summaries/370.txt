Omar v Gordhanbhai and another

Division: High Court of Kenya at Nairobi

Date of judgment: 13 November 1974

Case Number: 1820/1972 (13/75)

Before: Harris J

Before: Harris J

Sourced by: LawAfrica

[1] Civil Practice and Procedure  Interrogatories  Principles on which allowed.

Editors Summary

The plaintiff in a running-down action applied for leave to deliver interrogatories concerning the

conditions under which the accident happened and the plaintiffs injuries and damage.

Held 

(i) interrogatories may establish facts but not evidence and what case may be met;

( ii) the questions concerning the conditions under which the accident happened would be allowed.

Order accordingly.
