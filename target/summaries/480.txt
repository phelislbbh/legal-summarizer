Uganda v Matovu

Division: High Court of Uganda at Kampala

Date of judgment: 6 October 1973

Date of judgment: 6 October 1973

Case Number: 164/1973 (11/74)

Before: Wambuzi CJ

Sourced by: LawAfrica

[1] Criminal Practice and Procedure  Compensation  Accused must be heard.

[2] Criminal Law  Theft  Same offence as theft by servant.

Editors Summary

The accused was convicted of theft, although the evidence showed that he stole as a servant. Without opportunity to be heard, he was ordered to make restitution.

Held 

(i) Theft by a servant is not a different offence; it merely carries a heavier penalty;

( ii) an opportunity for the accused to be heard must be given before the making of an order for restitution.

Order set aside.
